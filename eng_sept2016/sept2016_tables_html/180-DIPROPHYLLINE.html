DIPROPHYLLINE

RxNorm: 3714

ATC:

R03DA01 R03DB01

PROBENECID

RxNorm: 8698

ATC: M04AB01

Risk of overdose due to increase of the plasma concentrations of diprophylline (inhibition of its renal tubular secretion)

Precaution for use

Reduce the dosage of diprophylline during the treatment with the probenicid
