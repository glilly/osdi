CLONAZEPAM

RxNorm: 2598

ATC: N03AE01

CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the active metabolite of the carbamazepine. In addition, decrease of the plasma concentrations of the clonazepam due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the carbamazepine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, plasma dosages, and possible adjustment of the dosages of the two anticonvulsants
