TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

MINERALOCORTICOIDS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 160

MINERALOCORTICOIDS

CLASS CODE:

H02AA0

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Decrease of the plasma concentrations and of the effectiveness of the corticoids due to increase of their hepatic metabolism by the inducer: the consequences are particularly great in patients with Addison’s disease treated with the hydrocortisone and in the case of transplants.

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring; adjustment of the dosage of the corticoids during the treatment with the inducer and after it is stopped.
