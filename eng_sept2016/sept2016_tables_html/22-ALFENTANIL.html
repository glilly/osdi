CIMETIDINE

RxNorm: 2541

ATC: A02BA01

With cimetidine used in doses greater than or equal to 800mg/day: increase of the opioid analgesic's effect of respiratory depression due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Adjust the dosage of the alfentanil when administered with the cimetidine.
