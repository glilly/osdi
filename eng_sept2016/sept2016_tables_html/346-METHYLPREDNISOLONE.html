VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

For doses of 0.5g to 1 g of methylprednisolone administered in bolus: increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

Testing of the INR 2 to 4 days after the bolus of mythylprednisolone or in the presence of signs of hemorrhage
