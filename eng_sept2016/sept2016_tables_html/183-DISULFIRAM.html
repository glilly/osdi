DISULFIRAM

RxNorm: 3554

ATC: N07BB01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the disulfiram and a week after it is stopped
