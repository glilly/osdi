RxNorm: 6038

ATC: J04AC01

HALOGENATED HYDROCARBON ANESTHETICS

CLASS CODE:

N01AB0-001

Increase of the hepatotoxic effect of the isoniazid, with increased formation of its toxic metabolites.

.

Precaution for use

In case of a scheduled intervention, it is prudent to stop the treatment with the isoniazid a week before the surgery and only restart it two weeks after.

Described for the prednisolone.

Clinical and biological monitoring.

Strict clinical monitoring, plasma concentration dosage of phenytoin and possible adjustment of its dosage during the treatment with the isoniazid and after it is stopped.
