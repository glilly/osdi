ACITRETIN

Rx Norm: 16818

ATC: D05BB02

ALCOHOL (DRINK OR INGREDIENT)

With women of child bearing age, risk of transformation of the acitretin into etretinate, a powerful agent of birth defects and malformations with a very prolonged half life (120 days), exposing the woman to a major risk of these birth defects and malformations of the child in case of pregnancy, during the treatment and two months after it ends.
