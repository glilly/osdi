ACETYLSALICYLIC ACID

RxNorm: 1191

ATC:

A01AD05 B01AC06 N02BA01

Increase of the toxicity, especially hematological, of the methotrezate (decrease of its renal clearance by the acetylsalicylic acid)

With doses of methotrexate > 20 mg/week

Contraindication with acetylsalicylic acid used in anatalgic, antipyretic, or anti-inflammatory doses

Precaution for use

with anti-coagulating doses of acetylsalicylic acid. Weekly monitoring of the CBC during the first weeks that the substances are administered together. Increased monitoring in the case of change (even slight) of renal function, as well as with older patients

With methotrexate used in doses =< 20 mg/week:

Precaution for use

with acetylsalicylic acid in analgesic, antipyretic, or anti-inflammatory doses. Weekly monitoring of the CBC during the first weeks of administration of the substances together. Increased monitoring in case of change (even slight) in renal function, as well as with older patients.

NON STEROIDAL ANTI-INFLAMMATORY DRUGS

CLASS CODE:

M01A-002

Increase of the hematological toxicity of the methotrexate (decrease of the renal clearance of the methotrexate by the NSAIs).

Not recommended

--for doses of methotrexate greater than 20mg per week

--with the ketoprofen and the methotrexate in doses greater than 20 mg per week, wait at least 12 hours between stopping the methotrexate and the beginning of the treatment with ketoprofen and the dose of methotrexate

Precaution for use

--with the methotrexate used in week doses (lower than or equal to 20 mg per week), weekly testing of the CBC during the first weeks of the administration of these medications together. Increased monitoring in case of alteration (even slight) of the renal function, as well as with the older patient.
