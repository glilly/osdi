Increase of the plasma concentrations of dabigatran, with increase of the risk of bleeding

Precaution for use

In a post surgical indication: clinical monitoring and adjustment of the dosage of the dabigatran to 150mg/day in one dose, or even 75mg/day in case of moderate renal insufficiency.

In an atrial fibrillation indication: clinical monitoring and adjustment of the dosage of the dabigatran to 220 mg/day in two doses.
