TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

TIBOLONE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 218

TIBOLONE

RxNorm: 38260

ATC: G03CX01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the tibolone and after it is stopped.
