TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

ACETAZOLAMIDE

RxNorm: 167

ATC: S01EC01

Increase of the hyperammonemia, with accrued risk of encephalopathy

Precaution for use

Regular clinical and biological monitoring.

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

AZTREONAM

RxNorm: 1272

ATC: J01DF01

Risk of onset of convulsive crises, due to decrease of the plasma concentrations of the valproic acid

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, plasma dosages, and possible adjustment of the dosage of the anticonvulsant during the treatment with the antibiotic and after it is stopped.

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the active metabolite of the carbamazepine with signs of overdose. In addition, decrease of the plasma concentrations of valproic acid due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the carbamazepine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, plasma dosages and adjustment of the dosage of the two anticonvulsants.

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

FELBAMATE

RxNorm: 24812

ATC: N03AX10

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the valproic acid, with risk of overdose.

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, and possible adjustment of the dosage of the valproic acid or of the valpromide during the treatment with the felbamate and after it is stopped.

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

LAMOTRIGINE

RxNorm: 28439

ATC: N03AX09

Increased risk of serious cutaneous reactions (Lyell's syndorme). In addition, increase of the plasma concentrations of lamotrigine (decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the sodium valproate)

Not recommended

If this combination of medications is judged necessary, strict clinical monitoring.

Take into account

ZIDOVUDINE (AZT)

RxNorm: 11413

ATC:

J05AF01 J05AR01 J05AR04 J05AR05

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects, especially hematological ones, of the zidovudine due to decrease of its metabolism by the valproic acid.

Precaution for use

Regular clinical and biological monitoring. A CBC checking for anemia should be done during the course of the first two months of the administration of these substances together.
