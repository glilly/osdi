TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 122

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

FUSIDIC ACID

RxNorm: 4608

ATC:

D06AX01 D09AA02 J01XC01 S01AA13

Increased risk of undesirable effects (concentration-dependent) of the rhabdomyolysis type.

CONTRAINDICATION

Stop the treatment with the HMG Co-A reductase inhibitor before starting a treatment with fusidic acid or use another antibiotic

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

COLCHICINE

RxNorm: 2683

ATC: M04AC01

Risk of increase of the undesirable muscular effects of these substances, and especially of rhabdomyolysis

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, especially at the beginning of the administration of these substances together.

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

DAPTOMYCIN

RxNorm: 22299

ATC: J01XX09

Risk of addition of the undesirable effects (dose dependent) of the rhabdomyolysis type.

Not recommended

If the administration of these medications together cannot be avoided, reinforce biological monitoring (dosage of the CPK more than once a week) and strict clinical monitoring.

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

ELTROMBOPAG

RxNorm: 711942

ATC: B02BX05

Risk of increase of the toxicity of the statins, due to inhibition of their hepatic recapture

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of the statin.

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

FIBRATES

CLASS CODE:

C01AB-001

Risk of addition of undesirable effects (dose-dependent) of the rhabdomyolosis type. Furthermore, with the gemfibrozil, decrease of the metabolism of the simvastatin and of the rosuvastatin, which increases the muscular risk, as well as the nephrotoxicity of the rosuvastatin

Contraindication

--with the gemfibrosil and the simvastatin

--for doses of 40mg of rosuvastatin

Not recommended

--with the other statins and the fibrates (except gemfibrosil/simvastatin

--with rosuvastatin <40mg

--do not exceed 10mg of simvastatin, except with the fenofibrate

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

LEDIPASVIR

RxNorm: 1591922

ATC: not found

Risk of increase of the plasma concentrations of the statin and of its undesirable effects of the rhabdomyolosis type.

Contraindication

--with the rosuvastatin.

Precaution for use

--with the other HMG-CoA reductase inhibitors.

Clinical and biological monitoring. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the statin.

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

LENALIDOMIDE

RxNorm: 342369

ATC: L04AX04

Increased risk of the onset of rhabdomyolysis

Precaution for use

Reinforce clinical and biological monitoring, especially during the first weeks of treatment.
