LITHIUM

RxNorm: 6448

ATC: D11AX04

Risk of reduction of the effectiveness of the lithium due to increase of its renal elimination by the sodium salts.

Precaution for use

Avoid excess amounts of sodium and take into account the presence of sodium in certain medications such as antacids.
