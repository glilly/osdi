TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

THIAZIDE DIURETICS AND RELATED

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 82

THIAZIDE DIURETICS AND RELATED

CLASS CODE:

C03-003

CALCIUM

RxNorm: 1428011

ATC: A12AA

Risk of hypercalcemia due to decrease of the urinary elimination of the calcium.

Take into account

THIAZIDE DIURETICS AND RELATED

CLASS CODE:

C03-003

LITHIUM

RxNorm: 6448

ATC: D11AX04

Increase of the plasma concentrations of lithium with signs of overdose of lithium, as with a reduced sodium regimen (decrease of the urinary excretion of the lithium)

Not recommended

If administering the substances together cannot be avoided, strict monitoring of the plasma concentrations of lithium and adjustment of the dosage of the lithium.
