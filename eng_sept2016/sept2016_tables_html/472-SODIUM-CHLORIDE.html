LITHIUM

RxNorm: 6448

ATC: D11AX04

Risk of reduction of the effectiveness of the lithium due to increase of its renal elimination by the sodium salts

Precaution for use

Avoid excessive sodium and take into account the presence of sodium in some medications such as antacids.
