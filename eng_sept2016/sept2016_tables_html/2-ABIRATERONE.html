FLECAINIDE

RxNorm: 4441

ATC: C01BC04

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the flecainide, due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the abiraterone.

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring. If needed, adjustment of the dosage of the flecainide during the treatment with the abiraterone
