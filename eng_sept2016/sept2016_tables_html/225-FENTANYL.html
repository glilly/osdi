N01AH01 N02AB03

STRONG INHIBITORS OF CYP3A4

CLASS CODE:

J02-J05

Risk of increase of the opioid analgesic's effect of respiratory depression due to slight decrease of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and adjustment of the dosage of the opioid analgesic when there is treatment with a strong inhibitor of CYP3A4.
