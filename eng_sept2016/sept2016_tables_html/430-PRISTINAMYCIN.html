TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

PRISTINAMYCIN

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of

September 2016, pp. 181-182

PRISTINAMYCIN

RxNorm: 66958

ATC: J01FG01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the pristinamycin and after it is stopped

PRISTINAMYCIN

RxNorm: 66958

ATC: J01FG01

COLCHICINE

RxNorm: 2683

ATC: M04AC01

Increase of the undesirable effects of the colchicine with potentially fatal consequences

CONTRAINDICATION

PRISTINAMYCIN

RxNorm: 66958

ATC: J01FG01

IMMUNOSUPPRESSANTS

CLASS CODE:

L04A

Increase of the blood concentrations of the immunosuppressant due to inhibition of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of the immunosuppressant, testing of the renal function and adjustment of its dosage during the administration of the medications together and after it is stopped.
