IDELALISIB

RxNorm: 1544460

ATC: L01XX47

CALCIUM CHANNEL BLOCKERS

CLASS CODE:

CO8CA-001

Increase of the undesirable effects of the calcium channel blocker, of the orthostatic hypotension sort, especially with older patients

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and adjustment of the dosage of the calcium channel blocker during the treatment with idelalisib and after it is stopped.
