APOMORPHINE

RxNorm: 1043

ATC:

G04BE07 N04BC07

Severe hypotension and loss of consciousness have been reported when ondansetron is administered with apomorphine

CONTRAINDICATION

ONDANSÉTRON

RxNorm: 26225

ATC: A04AA01

SUBSTANCES THAT TEND TO INDUCE TORSADES DE POINTES

CLASS CODE:

C01-N05

Increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias, especially of torsades de pointes

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and EKG during the administration of these medications together.
