DOXYCYCLINE

RxNorm: 3640

ATC: A01AB22 J01AA02

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Decrease of the plasma concentations of the doxycycline due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the inducer

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of the doxycycline.
