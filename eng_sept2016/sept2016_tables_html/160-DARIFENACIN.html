DARIFENACIN

RxNorm: 136198

ATC: G04BD10

FLECAINIDE

RxNorm: 4441

ATC: C01BC04

Increase of the plasma concentrations of felcainide, with risk of overdose, due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the darifenacin

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and reduction of the dosage of the flecainide during the treatment with darifenacin.
