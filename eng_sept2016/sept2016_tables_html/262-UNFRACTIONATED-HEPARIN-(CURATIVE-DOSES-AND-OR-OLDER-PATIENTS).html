UNFRACTIONATED HEPARIN (CURATIVE DOSES AND/OR OLDER PATIENTS)

CLASS CODE:

B01AB-003

ACETYLSALICYLIC ACID

RxNorm: 1191

ATC:

A01AD05 B01AC06 N02BA01

Increase of the risk of hemorrhage (inhibition of the platelet function and irritation of the gastroduodenal mucosa by the acetylsalicylic acid)

Not recommended with

-anti-inflammatory doses of acetylsalicylic acid (>=1gr per dose and/or >=3 gr per day)

-analgesic or antipyretic doses (>=500 mg per dose and/or <3gr per day)

Use another anti-inflammatory or another analgesic or antipyretic

Take into account with

-anti-coagulating doses (of 50 mg to 375 mg per day)
