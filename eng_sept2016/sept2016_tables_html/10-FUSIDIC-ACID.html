FUSIDIC ACID

RxNorm: 4608

ATC:

D06AX01 D09AA02 J01XC01 S01AA13

HMG-COA REDUCTASE INHIBITORS (STATINS)

CLASS CODE:

C10AA

Increased risk of undesirable effects (concentration dependent) of the rhabdomyolysis type.

CONTRAINDICATION

Stop the treatment with the HMG-COA Reductase Inhibitor before starting a treatment with fusidic acid or use another antibiotic.
