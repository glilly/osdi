FLECAINIDE

RxNorm: 4441

ATC: C01BC04

Increase of the plasma concentrations of flecainide with risk of overdose, due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the duloxetine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and reduction of the dosage of the flecainide during the treatment with the duloxetine and after it is stopped.
