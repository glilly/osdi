VINCA ALKALOIDS

CLASS CODE:

L01CA

CLARITHROMYCIN

RxNorm: 21212

ATC:

J01FA09 A02BD04 A02BD05 A02BD06 A02BD07

Risk of increase of the toxicity of the antimitotic due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the clarithromycin

Precaution for use

Strict clinical and biological monitoring. Possibly, use another antibiotic.
