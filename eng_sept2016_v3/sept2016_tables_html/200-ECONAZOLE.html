TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

ECONAZOLE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 86

ECONAZOLE

RxNorm: 3743

ATC: D01AC03 G01AF05

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Regardless of the route of administration of the econazole:

increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with econazole and after it is stopped
