ANGIOTENSIN CONVERTING ENZYME INHIBITORS (ACE INHIBITORS)

CLASS CODE:

C09AA

With the gold salts administered by IV route: risk of "nitritoid" reaction when the ACE inhibitor is introduced (nausea, vomiting, vasomotor effects of the flushing type, hypotension, possibly sudden drop in blood pressure)
