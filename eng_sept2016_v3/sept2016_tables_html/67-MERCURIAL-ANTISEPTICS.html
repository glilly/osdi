TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

MERCURIAL ANTISEPTICS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 29

MERCURIAL ANTISEPTICS

CLASS CODE:

D08AK0

POVIDONE-IODINE

RxNorm: 8611

ATC:

D08AG02 D09AA09 D11AC06 G01AX11 R02AA15 S01AX18

Erythema, blisters, even necrosis of skin and mucosa (formation of a caustic complex in the case of concomitant use of mercurials and iodine antiseptics). The interaction depends on the stability of the organo-mercurial at the cutaneous level and on individual sensitivity.

Not recommended
