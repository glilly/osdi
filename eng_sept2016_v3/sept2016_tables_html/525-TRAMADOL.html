RxNorm: 10689

ATC: N02AX02

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Risk of increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the tramadol and after it is stopped.
