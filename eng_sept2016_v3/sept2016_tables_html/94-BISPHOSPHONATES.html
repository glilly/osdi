TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

BISPHOSPHONATES

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 43

BISPHOSPHONATES

CLASS CODE:

M05B-001

CALCIUM

RxNorm: 1428011

ATC: A12AA

For calcium salts administered via oral route: decrease of the digestive absorption of the bisphosphonates

Precaution for use

Take the calcium salts and antacids at a different time from the bisphosphonates (from 30 minutes minimum to more than 2 hours apart, if possible, depending on the bisphosphonate).
