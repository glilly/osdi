GLUCOSAMINE

RxNorm: 4845

ATC: M01AX05

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE: B01AA-001

Increase of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the Vitamin K Antagonist
