ANTICONVULSANT ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Decrease of the plasma concentrations of the metronidazole due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the inducer

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of metronidazole during the treatment with the inducer and after it is stopped.
