SULFASALAZINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 208

SULFASALAZINE

RxNorm: 9524

ATC: A07EC01

DIGOXIN

RxNorm: 3407

ATC: C01AA05

Decrease of the digoxinemia that can reach 50%

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, EKG, and, possibly, monitoring of the digoxinemia. If there is reason for it, adjustment of the dosage of the digoxin during the treatment with the sulfasalazine and after it is stopped.
