MONOCLONAL ANTIBODIES (EXCEPT ANTI-TNF ALPHA)

CLASS CODE:

MONANT

ATTENUATED LIVE VIRUS VACCINES

CLASS CODE:

J07B-001

Risk of general vaccine disease, possibly fatal.

Not recommended with:

--belimumab, blinatumomab, canakinumab, obinutuzumab, ofatumumab, rituximab, tocilizumab, ustekinumab

Take into account with:

--bevacizumab, brentuximab, cetuximab, denosumab, ibritumomab, ipilimumab, natalizumab, nivolumab, panitumumab, pembrolizumab, ramucirumab, trastuzumab, vedolizumab
