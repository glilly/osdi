CALCITONIN

RxNorm: 36118

ATC:

H05BA H05BA03 H05BA02 H05BA01

LITHIUM

RxNorm: 6448

ATC: D11AX04

Risk of reduction of the effectiveness of the lithium due to increase of its renal elimination by the calcitonin

Precaution for use

Regular clinical and biological monitoring, especially at the beginning of the administration of the two substances together.
