TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

COLCHICINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 64

COLCHICINE

RxNorm: 2683

ATC: M04AC01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the colchicine and a week after it is stopped.

COLCHICINE

RxNorm: 2683

ATC: M04AC01

CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC: L04AD01 S01XA18

Risk of addition of the undesirable neuromuscular effects and increase of the toxicity of the colchicine with risk of overdose due to inhibition of its elimination by the ciclosporin, espceially in cases of pre-existing renal insufficiency

Not recommended

COLCHICINE

RxNorm: 2683

ATC: M04AC01

FIBRATES

CLASS CODE:

C01AB-001

Risk of increase of the undesirable muscular effects of these substances, and especially of rhabdomyolysis

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, particularly at the beginning of the administration of these substances together.

Not recommended

STRONG INHIBITORS OF CYP3A4

CLASS CODE:

J02-J05

Increase of the undesirable effects of the colchicine, with potentially fatal consequences

Contraindication:

--with the macrolides

Not recommended:

--with the azole antifungals, the ritonavir boosted protease inhibitors, and cobocistat
