J05AB06 S01AD09

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the didanosine, and especially the mitochondrial toxicity, due to substantial increase of its concentrations. Not recommended

PENTAMIDINE

RxNorm: 7994

ATC: P01CX01

Increased risk of onset of pancreatitis due to addition of undesirable effects

Precaution for use

Monitoring of the blood amylase level. Do not use these two medications together if the blood amylase level is at the high range of normal.
