ST JOHNS WORT

Hypericum perforatum

RxNorm: 258326

ATC : not found

Decrease of the digoxinemia, by reason of the enzyme inducer effect of the St Johns wort, with risk of reduction of effectiveness, even complete loss of the effect, which can have possibly serious consequences (acute decompensated heart failure).

CONTRAINDICATION

In the case of the two substances being taken together by accident, do not stop the St Johns wort abruptly, but test the plasma concentrations (or the effectiveness) of the digoxin before and then after the St Johns wort is stopped.
