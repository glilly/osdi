CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of carbamazepine with signs of overdose

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, and, if needed, testing of the plasma concentrations of carbamazepine and possible reduction of its dosage.

LITHIUM

RxNorm: 6448

ATC: D11AX04

Decrease of the plasma concentrations of lithium with risk of reduction of the therapeutic effectiveness

Precaution for use

Strict monitoring of the plasma concentrations of lithium and possible adjustment of the dosage of the lithium.
