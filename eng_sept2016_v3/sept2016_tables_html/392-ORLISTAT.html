VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the orlistat and after it is stopped.

CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC:

L04AD01 S01XA18

Decrease of the blood concentrations of ciclosporin due to decrease of its intestinal absorption, with risk of loss of the immunosuppressive activity

Not recommended

Take the orlistat at a different time from the ciclosporin (at least three hours apart). Reinforce testing of the blood dosages of ciclosporin, especially at the beginning of the administration of the two medications together, and at the time of a possible increase of the dosage of the orlistat.
