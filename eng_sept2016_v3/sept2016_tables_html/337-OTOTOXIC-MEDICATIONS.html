OTOTOXIC MEDICATIONS

The combined use of ototoxic medications increases the risk of cochleovestibular problems. If such a combination of medications is required, it is necessary to strengthen testing of the auditory function.

The medications concerned are, especially, the glycopeptides such as vancomycin and teicoplanin, the aminoglycosides, the platinum based antineoplastic drugs, and the loop diuretics.

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 149
