TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

IODINATED X-RAY CONTRAST MEDIA

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 182

IODINATED X-RAY CONTRAST MEDIA

CLASS CODE:

VO8A-001

ALDESLEUKIN

RxNorm: 70223

ATC: L03AC01

Increase of the risk of reaction to the contrast media in cases of previous treatment with interleukin2:

skin rash or more rarely low blood pressure, oliguria or even renal failure

Take into account

IODINATED X-RAY CONTRAST MEDIA

CLASS CODE:

VO8A-001

DIURETICS

CLASS CODE:

C0E-001

In case of dehydration caused by the diuretics, increased risk of acute functional renal insufficiency, in particular when large doses of the contrast media are used

Precaution for use

Rehydrate before administration of the iodinated product

IODINATED X-RAY CONTRAST MEDIA

CLASS CODE:

VO8A-001

METFORMIN

RxNorm: 6809

ATC:

A10BA02 A10BD13 A10BD11 A10BD05 A10BD03 A10BD10 A10BD07 A10BD02 A10BD08

Lactic acidosis linked to elevated concentrations of metformin in relation to functional renal insufficiency induced by the radiological exam.

CONTRAINDICATION

The treatment with metformin should be suspended at the time of the radiological exam and only started again 2 days later.
