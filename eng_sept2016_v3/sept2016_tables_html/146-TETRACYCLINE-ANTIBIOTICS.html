TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

TETRACYCLINE ANTIBIOTICS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, pp. 65-66

TETRACYCLINE ANTIBIOTICS

CLASS CODE:

J01AA

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the tetracycline antibiotic and after it is stopped.

TETRACYCLINE ANTIBIOTICS

CLASS CODE:

J01AA

CALCIUM

RxNorm: 1428011

ATC: A12AA

Decrease of the digestive absorption of the tetracycline antibiotics

Precaution for use

Take the calcium salts at a different time from the tetracycline antibiotic (more than two hours apart, if possible).

TETRACYCLINE ANTIBIOTICS

CLASS CODE:

J01AA

IRON

RxNorm: 1431589

ATC: B03A

With the tetracycline antibiotics administered by oral route: decrease of the digestive absorption of the tetracycline antibiotics (formation of complexes)

Precaution for use

Take the iron salts at a different time from the tetracycline antibiotics (more than 2 hours apart, if possible).
