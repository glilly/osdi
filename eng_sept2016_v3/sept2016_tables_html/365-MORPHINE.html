RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Decrease of the plasma concentrations and of the effectiveness of the morphine and of its active metabolite

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of the morphine during the treatment with the rifampicin and after it is stopped.
