RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Risk of decrease of the plasma concentrations of the haloperidol and of its therapeutic effectiveness, due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the rifampicin

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, and, if needed, adjustment of the dosage during the treatment with the rifampicin and after it is stopped.
