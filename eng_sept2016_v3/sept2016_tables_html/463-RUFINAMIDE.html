TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

RUFINAMIDE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 198

RUFINAMIDE

RxNorm: 69036

ATC: N03AF03

COMBINED ESTROGEN-PROGESTIN CONTRACEPTIVES

CLASS CODE:

G03AB-001

Moderate decrease of the concentrations of ethinylestradiol

Precaution for use

Another contraceptive method, in particular a barrier type, should be used.

RUFINAMIDE

RxNorm: 69036

ATC: N03AF03

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

Possible increase of the concentrations of rufinamide, especially with children who weigh less than 30 kg.

Precaution for use

With children who weigh less than 30 kg: do not exceed the total dose of 600mg/day after the period of titration.
