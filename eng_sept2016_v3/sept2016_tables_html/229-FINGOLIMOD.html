FINGOLIMOD

RxNorm: 1012892

ATC: L04AA27

BRADYCARDIA INDUCING DRUGS

CLASS CODE:

C0N0S0

Increase of the bradycardia inducing effects which can have fatal consequences. The beta-blocking agents are even more at risk since they inhibit the adrenergic compensation mechanisms

Not recommended

Clinical monitoring and continuous EKG during the 24 hours following the first dose.
