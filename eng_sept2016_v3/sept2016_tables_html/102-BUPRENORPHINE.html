BENZODIAZEPINES AND RELATED

CLASS CODE:

N05-001

With the buprenorphine used in opioid replacement therapy: increased risk of respiratory depression which could be fatal.

Take into account

Evaluate carefully the risk/benefits relationship of administering these substances together. Inform the patient of the necessity of adhering to the prescribed dosages.
