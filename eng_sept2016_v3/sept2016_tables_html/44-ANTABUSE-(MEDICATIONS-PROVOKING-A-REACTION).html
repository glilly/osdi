TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

ANTABUSE (MEDICATIONS PROVOKING A REACTION)

Medications that provoke an antabuse reaction with alcohol are numerous, and their administration with alcohol is not recommended.

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 16

ANTABUSE (REACTION)

CLASS CODE:

123-ANT

ALCOHOL

(DRINK OR INGREDIENT)

Antabuse effect (heat, redness, vomiting, tachycardia)

Not recommended

Avoid taking alcoholic beverages and medications containing alcohol. Take into account the complete elimination of the medications by referring to their half-life before re-starting alcoholic beverages or medications containing alcohol.
