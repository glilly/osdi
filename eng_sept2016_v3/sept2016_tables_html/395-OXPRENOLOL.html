TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

OXPRENOLOL

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 170

OXPRENOLOL

RxNorm: 7801

ATC: C07AA02

ERGOTAMINE

RxNorm: 4025

ATC: N02CA02

Ergotism: some cases of arterial spasm with ischemia of the extremities have been observed (addition of vascular effects)

Precaution for use

Reinforce clinical monitoring, in particular during the first weeks of the administration of these substances together.
