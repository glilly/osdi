TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

RANOLAZINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of

September 2016, pp. 187-188

RANOLAZINE

RxNorm: 35829

ATC: C01EB18

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYME INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Risk of large decrease of the concentrations of ranolazine

Not recommended

RANOLAZINE

RxNorm: 35829

ATC: C01EB18

ATORVASTATIN

RxNorm: 83367

ATC: C10AA05

Increased risk of undesirable effects (concentration-dependant) of the rhabodmyolysis type due to inhibition of the metabolism of the atorvastatin by the ranolazine

Precaution for use

Use weaker doses of the cholesterol lowering agent or another statin not affected by this type of interaction.

RANOLAZINE

RxNorm: 35829

ATC: C01EB18

DIGOXIN

RxNorm: 3407

ATC: C01AA05

Increase of the digoxinemia

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, and possibly EKG. Adjustment of the dosage of the digoxin if needed.

RANOLAZINE

RxNorm: 35829

ATC: C01EB18

IMMUNOSUPPRESSANTS

CLASS CODE:

L04A

Increase of the blood concentrations of the immunosuppressant due to inhibition of its metabolism by the ranolazine

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, and possible adjustment of the dosage of the immunosuppressant.

RANOLAZINE

RxNorm: 35829

ATC: C01EB18

STRONG INHIBITORS OF CYP3A4

CLASS CODE:

J02-J05

Increase of the concentrations of ranolazine due to decrease of its metabolism by the inhibitor

SIMVASTATIN

RxNorm: 36567

Increased risk of undesirable effects (concentration-dependent) of the rhabdomyolysis type due to inhibition of the metabolism of the simvastatin by the ranolazine
