CLASS CODE:

N06AB

IRREVERSIBLE MONOAMINE OXIDASE INHIBITORS (MAOI)

CLASS CODE:

N06AF0

Risk of appearance of a serotonin syndrome: diarrhea, tachycardia, sweats, trembling, confusion, even coma

CONTRAINDICATION

Wait at least two weeks between the discontinuation of the irreversible MAOI and the start of treatment with the SSRI, and at least a week between the end of treatment with the SSRI (except fluoxetine: five weeks) and the beginning.
