RACECADOTRIL

RACECADOTRIL

RxNorm: 16738

ATC: A07XA04

ANGIOTENSIN CONVERTING ENZYME INHIBITORS (ACE INHIBITORS)

CLASS CODE:

C09AA

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the angioedema type.

Contraindication

-in case of previous angioedema while taking ACE inhibitors

Not recommended

--in the absence of previous angioedema with ACE inhibitors.
