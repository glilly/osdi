TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

SULFINPYRAZONE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 208

SULFINPYRAZONE

RxNorm: 10205

ATC: M04AB02

CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC:

L04AD01 S01XA18

Decrease of the blood concentrations of ciclosporin due to increase of its metabolism by the sulfinpyrazone

Precaution for use

Testing of blood concentrations of ciclosporin and possible adjustment of its dosage during the treatment with sulfinpyrazone and after it is stopped.
