TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

FLUOROQUINOLONES

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 99

FLUOROQUINOLONES

CLASS CODE:

J01MA-001

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the fluoroquinolone and after it is stopped.

FLUOROQUINOLONES

CLASS CODE:

J01MA-001

IRON

RxNorm: 1431589

ATC: B03A

Decrease of the digestive absorption of the fluoroquinolones

Precaution for use

Take the iron salts at a different time from the fluoroquinolones (more that 2 hours apart if possible).

GLUCOCORTICOIDS (EXCEPT HYDROCORTISONE)

Possible increase of the risk of tendinitis, even of rupture of the tendon (exceptional), particularly in patients receiving prolonged corticotherapy
