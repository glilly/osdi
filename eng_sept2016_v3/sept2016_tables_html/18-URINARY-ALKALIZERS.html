TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

URINARY ALKALIZERS

from the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 7

URINARY ALKALIZERS

CLASS CODE:

B05-001

HYDROQUINIDINE

RxNorm: 27220

ATC: not found

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the hydroquinidine and risk of overdose (decrease of the renal excretion of the hydroquinidine by alkalinizing of the urine)

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, EKG, and possibly testing of the blood concentrations of the hydroquinidine; if needed, adjustment of the dosage during the alkalinizing treatment and after it is stopped.

B05-001

QUINIDINE

RxNorm: 9068

ATC: C01BA01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the quinidine and risk of overdose (decrease of the renal excretion of the quinidine by alkalinizing of the urine).
