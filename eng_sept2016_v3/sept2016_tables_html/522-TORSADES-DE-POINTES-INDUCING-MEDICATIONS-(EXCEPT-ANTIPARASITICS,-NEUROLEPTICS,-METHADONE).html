TORSADES DE POINTES INDUCING MEDICATIONS (EXCEPT ANTIPARASITICS, NEUROLEPTICS, METHADONE)

CLASS CODE:

C01B

SUBSTANCES THAT TEND TO INDUCE TORSADES DE POINTES

CLASS CODE:

C01-N05

Increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias, especially of torsades de pointes

Contraindication

--for erythromycin and vincamine, only the forms administered by IV route are affected by this interaction.

--for spiramycin, the IV and oral routes are concerned

Not recommended

--with the antiparasitics (chloroquine halofantrine, lumefantrine, pentamidine), the neuroleptics, methadone and hydroxychloroquine
