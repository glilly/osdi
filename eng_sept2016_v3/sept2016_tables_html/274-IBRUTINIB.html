TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

IBRUTINIB

See also: metabolized tyrosine-kinase inhibitors

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 110

IBRUTINIB

RxNorm: 1442981

ATC: L01XE27

AMIODARONE

RxNorm: 703

ATC: C01BD01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of ibrutinib due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the amiodarone

Precaution for use

Strict clinical monitoring and reduction of the dose of ibrutinib to 140 mg per day during the length of time these medications are administered together.
