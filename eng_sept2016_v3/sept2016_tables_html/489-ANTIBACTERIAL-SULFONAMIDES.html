ANTIBACTERIAL SULFONAMIDES

CLASS CODE:

JO1E

METHOTREXATE

RxNorm: 6851

ATC:

L01BA01 L04AX03

Increase of the hematological toxicity of the methotrexate

Precaution for use

Concentration dosage of methotrexate. Adjustment of the dosage if necessary during the administration of the substances together and after it is stopped.
