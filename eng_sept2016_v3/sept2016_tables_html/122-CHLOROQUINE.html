CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC:

L04AD01 S01XA18

Risk of increase of the blood concentrations of ciclosporin and of the creatinemia

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of ciclosporin, testing of the renal function, and adjustment of the dosage during the administration of the substances together and after the chloroquine is stopped.

THYROID HORMONES

CLASS CODE:

H03AA

Risk of clinical hypothyroidism in patients taking thyroid hormones in substitution therapy

Precaution for use

Monitoring of serum concentrations of T3 and T4 and adjustment, if needed, of the dosage of the thyroid hormone during the treatment with the antimalarial and after it is stopped.
