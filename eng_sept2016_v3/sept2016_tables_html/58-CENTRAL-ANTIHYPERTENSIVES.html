TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

CENTRAL ANTIHYPERTENSIVES

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 25

CENTRAL ANTIHYPERTENSIVES

CLASS CODE:

C02AC-001

BETA BLOCKING AGENTS (EXCEPT ESMOLOL)

CLASS CODE:

C07AB-002

Large increase of blood pressure in cases of sudden termination of the treatment with the central antihypertensive

Precaution for use

Avoid ending treatment with the central antihypertensive abruptly. Clinical monitoring.

CENTRAL ANTIHYPERTENSIVES

CLASS CODE:

C02AC-001

BETA-BLOCKING AGENTS IN HEART FAILURE

CLASS CODE:

C07AB-003

Central decrease of the sympathetic tone and vasodilator effect of the central antihypertensives, dangerous in case of heart failure treated with a beta-blocking agent and vasodilator

Not recommended
