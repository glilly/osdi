TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

METYRAPONE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 154

METYRAPONE

RxNorm: 6923

ATC: V04CD01

PHENYTOIN (AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, FOSPHENYTOIN)

CLASS CODE:

N03AB

Risk of false negative results of the test with metyrapone, due to a decrease of its plasma concentrations, because of increase of its hepatic metabolism by the phenytoin.

Precaution for use

Double the dosage of the metyrapone.
