AMIODARONE

RxNorm: 703

ATC: C01BD01

Increase of the blood concentrations of tacrolimus due to inhibition of its metabolism by the amiodarone

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of tacrolimus, testing of the renal function, and adjustment of the dosage of tacrolimus during its administration with the amiodarone and when the amiodarone is stopped.
