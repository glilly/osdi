MOCLOBEMIDE

RxNorm: 30121

ATC: N06AG02

CIMETIDINE

RxNorm: 2541

ATC: A02BA01

With the cimetidine used in doses greater than or equal to 800 mg/day: increase of the concentrations of the moclobemide, due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring with possible adjustment of the dosage of moclobemide
