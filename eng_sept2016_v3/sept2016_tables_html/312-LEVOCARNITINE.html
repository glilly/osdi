TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

LEVOCARNITINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 141

LEVOCARNITINE

RxNorm: 42955

ATC: A16AA01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE: B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the levocarnitine and a week after it is stopped.
