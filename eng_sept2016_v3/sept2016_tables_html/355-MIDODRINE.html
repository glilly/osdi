IRREVERSIBLE MONOAMINE OXIDASE INHIBITORS (MAOI)

CLASS CODE:

N06AF0

Hypertensive crises (inhibition of the metabolism of the pressor amines). Because of the length of action of the MAOI, this interaction is still possible two weeks after the MAOI is stopped.
