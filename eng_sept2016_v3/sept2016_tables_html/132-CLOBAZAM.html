CLOBAZAM

RxNorm: 21241

ATC: N05BA09

STIRIPENTOL

RxNorm: 37119

ATC: N03AX17

Increase of the plasma concentrations of these anticonvulsants, with risk of overdose, due to inhibition of their hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, plasma dosage, when that is possible, of the anticonvulsant taken with the stiripentol and possible adjustment of the dosage of the anticonvulsant taken with it as well.
