MIRABEGRON

MIRABEGRON

RxNorm: 1300786

ATC: G04BD12

FLECAINIDE

RxNorm: 4441

ATC: C01BC04

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the flecainide by decrease of its metabolism by the mirabegron

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible reduction of the dosage of the flecainide during the administration of these medications together.
