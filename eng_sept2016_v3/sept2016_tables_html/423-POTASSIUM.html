POTASSIUM

RxNorm: 8588

ATC: A12BA

ANGIOTENSIN II RECEPTOR ANTAGONISTS or ANGIOTENSIN RECEPTOR BLOCKERS (ARBs)

CLASS CODE:

C09CA0-001

Hyperkalemia (potentially lethal) especially when there is renal insufficiency (addition of the hyperkalemia inducing effects).

Not recommended

Avoid administering these substances together unless there is pre-existing hypokalemia.

POTASSIUM SPARING DIURETICS (ALONE OR IN COMBINATION)

CLASS CODE:

Not recommended:
