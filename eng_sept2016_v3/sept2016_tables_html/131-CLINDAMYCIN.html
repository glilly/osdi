VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the Vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage.

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the clindamycin and after it is stopped.
