HYPONATREMIA INDUCING MEDICATIONS

Some medications are more frequently implicated in the onset of hyponatremia. These are the diuretics, desmopressin, the selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors, carbamazine, and oxcarbazapine. Combining these medications increases the risk of hyponatremia
