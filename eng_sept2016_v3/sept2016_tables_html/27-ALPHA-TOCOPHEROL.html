VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

With the vitamin E used in doses greater than or equal to 500 mg per day: increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with vitamin E and after it is stopped.
