RISPERIDONE

RxNorm: 35636

ATC: N05AX08

CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Risk of decrease of the active fraction of the risperidone and of its therapeutic effectiveness due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the carbamazepine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, and, if needed, adjustment of the dosage of the risperidone.

FLUOXETINE

RxNorm: 4493

ATC: N06AB03

Increase of the active fraction of the risperidone due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the fluoxetine, with risk of increase of the undesirable effects

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, and, if needed, adjustment of the dosage of the risperidone.
