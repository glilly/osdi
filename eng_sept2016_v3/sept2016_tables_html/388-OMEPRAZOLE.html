DIGOXIN

RxNorm: 3407

ATC: C01AA05

Moderate increase of the digoxinemia due to increase of its absorption by the omeprazole

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, EKG, and monitoring of the digoxinemia, particularly with an older patient.

TACROLIMUS

RxNorm: 42316

ATC:

D11AH01 L04AD02

Increase of the blood concentrations of the tacrolimus

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of the tacrolimus, testing of the renal function and adjustment of the dosage during the administration of the substances together and after it is stopped.
