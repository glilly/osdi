TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

LINCOSANIDES

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 143

LINCOSANIDES

CLASS CODE:

J01FF0

CURARES

CLASS CODE:

M03A-001

Increase of the effects of the curares when the antibiotic is administered by parenteral and/or peritoneal route before, during, or after the curarizing agent

Precaution for use

Monitor the degree of curarisation at the end of anesthesia.
