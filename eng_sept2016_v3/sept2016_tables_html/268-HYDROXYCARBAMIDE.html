HYDROXYCARBAMIDE

RxNorm: 5552

ATC: L01XX05

ATTENUATED LIVE VIRUS VACCINES

CLASS CODE:

J07B-001

When indicated for a patient with drepanocytosis (sickle cell anemia), theoretical risk of general fatal vaccine disease.

Not recommended

Administering these together should be considered only if the benefits are estimated to be greater than the this risk.

If it is decided to interrupt the treatment with hydroxycarbamide in order to administer the vaccine, a wait of 3 months after stopping the hydroxycarbamide is recommended
