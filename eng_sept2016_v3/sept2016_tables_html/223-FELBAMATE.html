TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

FELBAMATE

See also: metabolized anticonvulsants

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 94

FELBAMATE

RxNorm: 24812

ATC: N03AX10

CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the active metabolite of the carbamazepine. In addition, decrease of the plasma concentrations of felbamate due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the carbamazepine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, plasma dosages, and possible adjustment of the dosages of the two anticonvulsants.

FELBAMATE

RxNorm: 24812

ATC: N03AX10

PHENOBARBITAL (AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, PRIMIDONE)

CLASS CODE:

N03AA-001

Decrease of the plasma concentrations and of the effectiveness of the felbamate and increase of the plasma concentrations of the phenobarbital, with risk of overdose.

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and testing of the plasma concentrations of phenobarbital with adjustment of the dosage if needed.

FELBAMATE

RxNorm: 24812

ATC: N03AX10

PHENYTOIN (AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, FOSPHENYTOIN)

CLASS CODE:

N03AB

Increase of the plasma concentrations of phenytoin with risk of overdose, due to inhibition of its metabolism by the felbamate

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, testing of the plasma concentrations of phenytoin, and if needed, adjustment of its dosage during the treatment with the felbamate.

FELBAMATE

RxNorm: 24812

ATC: N03AX10

VALPROIC (ACID) AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, VALPROMIDE

CLASS CODE:

N03AG0

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the valproic acid, with risk of overdose

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring, and possible adjustment of the dosage of the valproate or of the valpromide during the treatment with the felbamate and after it is stopped.
