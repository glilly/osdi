TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

CLASS IA ANTIARRHYTHMICS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 19

CLASS IA ANTIARRHYTHMICS

CLASS CODE:

C01B-002

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYME INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Decrease of the plasma concentrations and of the effectiveness of the antiarrhythmic, due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the inducer

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, EKG, and testing of the plasma concentrations; if needed, adjustment of the dosage of the antiarrhythmic during the treatment with the inducer and after it is stopped.

CLASS IA ANTIARRHYTHMICS

CLASS CODE:

C01B-002

ESMOLOL

RxNorm: 49737

ATC: C07AB09

Disorders of contractility, auto- regulation, and conduction (suppression of the compensatory sympathetic mechanisms)

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and EKG

CLASS IA ANTIARRHYTHMICS

CLASS CODE:

C01B-002

RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Decrease of the plasma concentrations and of the effectiveness of the antiarrhythmic (increase of its hepatic metabolism).

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, EKG, and possibly monitoring of the plasma concentrations of the antiarrhythmic. If needed, adjustment of the dosage of the antiarrhythmic during the treatment with the rifampicin and after it is stopped (risk of overdose of the antiarrhythmic)
