ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03-J05-001

Risk of decrease of the effectiveness of the hydrocortisone (increase of its metabolism); the consequences are serious when the hydrocortisone is administered in replacement therapy or in case of transplants

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring; adjustment of the dosage of the hydrocortisone during the time it is taken with the enzymatic inducer and after the enzymatic inducer is stopped.
