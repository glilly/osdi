VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Risk of increase of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the proguanil and after it is stopped.

THYROID HORMONES

CLASS CODE:

H03AA

Risk of clinically low thyroid with patients having thyroid replacement therapy

Precaution for use

Monitoring of the serum concentrations of T3 and T4 and adjustment, if needed, of the dosage of the thyroid hormone during the treatment with the antimalarial and after it is stopped.
