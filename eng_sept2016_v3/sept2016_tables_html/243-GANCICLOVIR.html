ATC:

J05AB06 S01AD09

DIDANOSINE

RxNorm: 3364

ATC: J05AF02

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the didanosine, and especially the mitochondrial toxicity, due to substantial increase of its concentrations. In addition, risk of decrease of the effectiveness of the ganciclovir due to decrease of its concentrations, if the two medications are ingested in an interval of less than 2 hours

Not recommended

ZALCITABINE

RxNorm: 3363

ATC: J05AF03

Increased risk of onset of peripheral neuropathy due to addition of undesirable effects

Precaution for use

Regular clinical and biological monitoring, especially at the beginning of the administration of the two medications together.

ZIDOVUDINE

RxNorm: 11413

ATC:

J05AF01 J05AR01 J05AR04 J05AR05

Increase of the hematological toxicity (addition of effects of bone marrow toxicity)

Precaution for use

Stop the zidovudine temporarily; CBC, and, reintroduce, if possible, the zidovudine in small doses.
