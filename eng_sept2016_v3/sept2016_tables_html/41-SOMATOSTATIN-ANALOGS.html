TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

SOMATOSTATIN ANALOGS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, pp. 15-16

SOMATOSTATIN ANALOGS

CLASS CODE:

H01CB0-001

CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC:

L04AD01 S01XA18

With the ciclosporin administered orally:

reduction of the blood concentrations of ciclosporin (decrease of its intestinal absorption).

Precaution for use

Increase the doses of ciclosporin according to testing of the plasma concentrations and reduction of the dosage after the treatment with the somatostatin analogue is stopped.

INSULIN

RxNorm: 5856

ATC:

A10AC01 A10AC02 A10AC03 A10AC04 A10AD01 A10AD02 A10AD03 A10AD04 A10AD05 A10AE01 A10AE02 A10AE03 A10AE04 A10AE05 A10AF01

Risk of hypoglycemia or hyperglycemia:

decrease or increase of the needs for insulin, due to decrease or increase of the secretion of endogenous glucagon

Precaution for use

Inform the patient of the risk of hypoglycemia or hyperclycemia, strengthen self- monitoring of blood glucose, and adjust the dosage of insulin if needed during the treatment with the somatostatin analog.
