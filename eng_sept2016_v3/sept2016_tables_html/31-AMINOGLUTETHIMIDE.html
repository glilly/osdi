TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

AMINOGLUTETHIMIDE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 11

AMINOGLUTETHIMIDE

RxNorm: 677

ATC: L02BG01

VITAMIN K ANTAGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Described for warfarin and acenocoumarol.

Decrease of the effect of the vitamin K antagonist, due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the aminoglutethimide

.

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the Vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with aminoglutethimide and two weeks after it is stopped.
