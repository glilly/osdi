IMMUNOSUPPRESSANTS

CLASS CODE:

L04A

Increase of the blood concentrations of the immunosuppressant due to inhibition of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of the immunosuppressant, testing of the renal function, and adjustment of the dosage during the administration of the substances together and after it is stopped.
