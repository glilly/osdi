CITRATES

CLASS CODE:

V09HX

ALUMINUM (SALTS)

CLASS CODE:

A02AD0-001

Risk of facilitation of systemic uptake of the aluminum, especially in case of altered renal function.

Precaution for use

Take the aluminum based gastrointestinal medications at a different time (more than 2 hours apart if possible) from the citrates, including natural citrates (citrus fruit juices).
