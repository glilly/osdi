NEPHROTOXIC MEDICATIONS

The combined use of medications having specific renal toxicity increases the risk of nephrotoxicity. If such a combination is necessary, it is imperative to strengthen biological monitoring of renal function.

The medications concerned are represented especially by the iodinated x-ray contrast media, aminoglycosides, platinum based antineoplastic cancer drugs, methotrexate in large doses, some antivirals (such as the "ciclovirs" or foscarnet), pentamidine, ciclosporin or tacrolimus.

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 149
