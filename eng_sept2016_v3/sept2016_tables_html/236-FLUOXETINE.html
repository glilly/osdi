CARBAMAZEPINE

RxNorm: 2002

ATC: N03AF01

Increase of the plasma concentrations of carbamazepine with signs of overdose

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring, testing of the plasma concentrations of carbamazepine, and possible reduction of the dosage of the carbamazepine during the treatment with the serotoninergic antidepressant and after it is stopped.

METOPROLOL

RxNorm: 6918

ATC: C07AB02

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the metoprolol, especially with excessive bradycardia, due to inhibition of its metabolism by the fluoxetine.

Precaution for use

Increased clinical monitoring, if needed, adjustment of the dosage of the metoprolol during the length of the treatment with the fluoxetine and after it is stopped.
