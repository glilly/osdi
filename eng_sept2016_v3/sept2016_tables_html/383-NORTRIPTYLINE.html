TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

NORTRIPTYLINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 166

NORTRIPTYLINE

RxNorm: 7531

ATC: N06AA10

BUPROPION

RxNorm: 42347

ACT: N06AX12

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the nortriptyline due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the bupropion

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring. If needed, adjustment of the dosage of the nortriptyline during the treatment with the bupropion.
