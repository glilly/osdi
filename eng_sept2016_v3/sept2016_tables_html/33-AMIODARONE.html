AMIODARONE

RxNorm : 703

ATC: C01BD01

AFATINIB

RxNorm: 1430438

ATC: L01XE13

Increase of the plasma concentrations of afatinib by increase of its absorption by the amiodarone.

Precaution for use

It is recommended that the amiodarone be administered as far as possible from the afatinib, preferably at an interval of 6 to 12 hours from the dose of afatinib.

CLASS CODE:

B01AA-001

Increase of the effect of the Vitamin K antagonist and of the risk of hemorrhage

Precaution for use

More frequent testing of the INR. Possible adjustment of the dosage of the vitamin K antagonist during the treatment with the amiodarone and a week after it is stopped.
