TIAGABINE

RxNorm: 31914

ATC: N03AG06

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYME INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Decrease of the plasma concentrations of the tiagabine due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the inducer

Precaution for use

An increase of the dosage of the tiagabine can prove necessary in cases when it is administered with an anticonvulsant enzyme inducer.
