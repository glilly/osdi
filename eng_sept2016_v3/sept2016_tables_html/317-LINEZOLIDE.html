RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Risk of decrease of the effectiveness of the linezolide due to increase of its hepatic metabolism by the rifampicin.

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible increase of the dosage of the linezolide during the treatment with the rifampicin.
