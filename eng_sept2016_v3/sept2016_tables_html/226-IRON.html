BISPHOSPHONATES

CLASS CODE:

M05B-001

For the iron salts administered via oral route: decrease of the digestive absorption of the bisphosphonates

Precaution for use

Take the iron salts at a different time from the bisphosphonates (from 30 minutes minimum to more than 2 hours apart, if possible, depending on the bisphosphonate).
