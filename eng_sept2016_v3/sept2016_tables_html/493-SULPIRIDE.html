SULPIRIDE

RxNorm: 10239

ATC: N05AL01

SUCRALFATE

RxNorm: 10156

ATC: A02BX02

Decrease of the digestive absorption of the sulpiride

Precaution for use

Take the sucralfate at a different time from the sulpiride (more than 2 hours apart, if possible).
