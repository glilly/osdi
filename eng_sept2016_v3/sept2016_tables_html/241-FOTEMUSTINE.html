FOTEMUSTINE

See also: cytotoxics

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of Sepember 2016, p. 102

FOTEMUSTINE

RxNorm: 25287

ATC: L01AD05

DACARBAZINE

RxNorm: 3098

ATC: L01AX04

With the dacarbazine in large doses: risk of pulmonary toxicity (adult acute respiratory distress syndrome ).

Precaution for use

Do not use these medications simultaneously, but wait a week between the last administration of fotemustine and the first day of the treatment with dacarbazine.
