BETA-2 MIMETICS or BETA2 ADRENERGIC AGONISTS

CLASS CODE:

R03C-001

HALOTHANE

RxNorm: 5095

ATC: N01AB01

In cases of obstetrical surgery, increased uterine inertia with risk of hemorrhage; furthermore serious ventricular arrhythmias, due to increase of the cardiac reactivity

Not recommended

Interrupt treatment with the beta-2 agonist if the anesthesia must be done with halothane
