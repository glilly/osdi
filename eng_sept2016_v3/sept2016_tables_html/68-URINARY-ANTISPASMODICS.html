TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

URINARY ANTISPASMODICS

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 30

URINARY ANTISPASMODICS

CLASS CODE:

DFST

STRONG INHIBITORS OF CYP3A4

CLASS CODE:

J02-J05

Risk of increase of undesirable effects

Contraindication

-with the darifenacin

-with the fesoterodine and the solifenacin, when there is renal or hepatic insufficiency, moderate to severe


Not recommended

-with the tolterodine

Precaution for use

-with the fesoterodine or the solifenacin, for the patient having normal renal and hepatic function, reduce the dose to 4mg or 5mg respectively, when administered with a strong inhibitor of CYP3A4

Take into account

-with the oxybutynin
