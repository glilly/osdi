HYDROXYCHLOROQUINE

RxNorm: 5521

ATC: P01BA02

METOPROLOL

RxNorm: 6918

ATC:

C07AB02 C07FB02 C07CB02 C07BB02

With the metoprolol used in heart failure: risk of increase of the undesirable effects of metoprolol due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the hydroxycloroquine

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring. If needed, adjustment of the dosage of the metoprolol during the treatment with the hydroxychloroquine

HYDROXYCHLOROQUINE

RxNorm: 5521

ATC: P01BA02

RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Risk of decrease of the therapeutic effect of the hydroxychloroquine, due to increase of its metabolism by the rifampicin

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of the hydroxychloroquine during the treatment with rifampicin and after it is stopped.

SUBSTANCES THAT TEND TO INDUCE TORSADES DE POINTES

CLASS CODE:

C01-N05

Increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias, especially of torsades de pointes

Contraindication

--with the citalopram, the domperidone, the escitalopram, the hydroxyzine and the piperaquine

Not recommended

--with the other medications that tend to induce torsades de pointes
