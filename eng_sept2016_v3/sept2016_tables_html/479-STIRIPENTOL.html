STIRIPENTOL

RxNorm: 37119

ATC: N03AX17

ANTICONVULSANT ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03A-001

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the anticonvulsant, with risk of overdose, due to inhibition of its hepatic metabolism by the stiripentol

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and plasma dosage, when that is possible, of the anticonvulsant taken with the stiripentol, and possible adjustment of its dosage.
