BORTEZOMIB

RxNorm: 358258

ATC: L01XX32

ENZYMATIC INDUCERS

CLASS CODE:

N03-J05-001

Decrease of the concentrations of the cytotoxic due to increase of its metabolism by the inducer, with risk of lower effectiveness

Take into account

BORTEZOMIB

RxNorm: 358258

ATC: L01XX32

STRONG INHIBITORS OF CYP3A4

CLASS CODE:

J02-J05

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects, especially neurological, of the bortezomib due to decrease of its metabolism.

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and possible adjustment of the dosage of the bortezomib during the length of the treatment with the enzymatic inhibitor.
