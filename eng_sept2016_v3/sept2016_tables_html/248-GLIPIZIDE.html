GLIPIZIDE

RxNorm: 4821

ATC: A10BB07

VORICONAZOLE

RxNorm: 121243

ATC: J02AC03

Risk of increase of the plasma concentrations of the glipizide at the origin of potentially severe hypoglycemia.

Precaution for use

Inform the patient, reinforce self-monitoring of blood glucose and possibly adjust the dosage of the blood glucose lowering sulfonamide during and after the treatment with voriconazole.
