PHENYTOIN (AND, BY EXTRAPOLATION, FOSPHENYTOIN)

CLASS CODE:

N03AB

Unpredictable variations: the plasma concentrations of phenytoin can increase, with signs of overdose, but also decrease or remain stable.

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and testing of the plasma concentrations of phenytoin.

STIRIPENTOL

RxNorm: 37119

ATC: N03AX17

Increase of the plasma concentrations of the diazepam, with risk of overdose, due to inhibition of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring and plasma dosage, when that is possible, of the anticonvulsant taken with the stiripentol and possible adjustment of the dosage of the anticonvulsant taken with it as well.
