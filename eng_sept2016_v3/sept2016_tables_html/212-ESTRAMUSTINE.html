ESTRAMUSTINE

RxNorm: 4089

ATC: L01XX11

CLODRONIC ACID

RxNorm: 3350

ATC: M05BA02

Risk of increase of the plasma concentrations of estramustine by the clodronate

Precaution for use

Clinical monitoring during the administration of these substances together.

ESTRAMUSTINE

RxNorm: 4089

ATC: L01XX11

CALCIUM

RxNorm: 1428011

ATC: A12AA

Decrease of the digestive absorption of the estramustine

Precaution for use

Take the calcium salts at a different time from the estramustine (more than 2 hours apart if possible).
