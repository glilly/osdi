TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

PYRIMETHAMINE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 185

PYRIMETHAMINE

RxNorm: 9010

ATC:

P01BD01 P01BF04

TRIMETHOPRIM

RxNorm: 10829

ATC: J01EA01

Megaloblastic anemia, more particularly with large doses of the two products (deficit of folic acid due to the combination of two 2-4 diaminopyrimidines)

Precaution for use

Regular CBC and addition of a treatment with folic acid (regular IM injections).
