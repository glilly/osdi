TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

PIOGLITAZONE

From the French ANSM drug interactions document of September 2016, p. 179

PIOGLITAZONE

RxNorm: 33738

ATC: A10BG03

SOMATOSTATIN ANALOGS

CLASS CODE: H01CB0-001

Risk of hyypoglycemia or hyperglycemia: decrease or increase of the need for glitazone, due to decrease of increase of the secretion of endogenous glucagon

Precaution for use

Increase self monitoring of blood glucose and adjust if needed the dosage of the glitazone during the treatment with the somatostatin analog

PIOGLITAZONE

RxNorm: 33738

ATC: A10BG03

GEMFIBROZIL

RxNorm: 4719

ATC: C10AB04

Risk of increase of the undesirable effects of the glitazone due to decrease of its hepatic metabolism by the gemfibrozil

Not recommended

If administering these medications together cannot be avoided, strict clinical monitoring.

PIOGLITAZONE

RxNorm: 33738

ATC: A10BG03

RIFAMPICIN

RxNorm: 9384

ATC: J04AB02

Decrease of the plasma concentrations of the glitazone due to increase of its metabolism by the rifampcin

Precaution for use

Clinical and biological monitoring; adjustment of the dosage of the glitazone during the treatment with the rifampicin and after it is stopped.
