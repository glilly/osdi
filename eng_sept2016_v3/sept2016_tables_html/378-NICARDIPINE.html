NICARDIPINE

RxNorm: 7396

ATC: C08CA04

IMMUNOSUPPRESSANTS

CLASS CODE:

L04A

Increase of the blood concentrations of the immunosuppressant, due to inhibition of its hepatic metabolism

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of the immunosuppressant, testing of the renal function, and adjustment of the dosage of the immunosuppressant during the treatment with nicardipine and after it is stopped.
