TABLE OF INTERACTIONS WITH

ATROPINIC MEDICATIONS

Account must be taken of the fact that atropinic substances can increase their undesirable effects and lead more easily to urine retention, a spike of glaucoma, constipation, dry mouth, etc.

The various atropinic medications are represented by the tricyclic antidepressants, most of the H1 blocking antihistamines, the antiparkinsonian anticholinergics, the atropinic antispasmodics, disopyramide, the phenothiazide neuroleptics, as well as clozapine.
