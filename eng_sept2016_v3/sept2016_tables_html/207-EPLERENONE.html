EPLERENONE

RxNorm: 298869

ATC: C03DA04

ANGIOTENSIN II RECEPTOR ANTAGONISTS or ANGIOTENSIN RECEPTOR BLOCKERS (ARBs)

CLASS CODE:

C09CA0-001

Increase of the risk of hyperkalemia, especially with older patients

Precaution for use

Strict testing of the kalemia and of the renal function during the administration of these substances together.
