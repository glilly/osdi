RxNorm: 60307

ATC: N04BX02

IRON

RxNorm: 1431589

ATC: B03A

Decrease of the digestive absorption of the entacapone and of the iron due to chelation of the latter by the entacapone

Precaution for use

Take the iron salts at a different time from the entacapone (more than 2 hours apart if possible).
