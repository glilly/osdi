DEFEROXAMINE

RxNorm: 3131

ATC: V03AC01

With large doses of ascorbic acid and by intravenous route:

cardiac function anomalies, even acute heart failure (generally reversible by ending the administration of the Vitamin C).

Precaution for use

In the case of hemochromatosis, only give Vitamin C after having started the treatment with the deferoxamine. Monitor cardiac function when these substances are administered together.
