CICLOSPORIN

RxNorm: 3008

ATC:

L04AD01 S01XA18

Risk of increase of the blood concentrations of ciclosporin and of the creatinemia

Precaution for use

Blood concentration dosage of the ciclosporin, testing of renal function, and adjustment of the dosage during the administration of the substances together and after the macrolide is stopped.
